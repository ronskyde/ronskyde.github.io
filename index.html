Welcome to Wings 23

Enjoy Rooftop Experience & We are inspired to serve you

The jewel in the crown of Eldoret, the city of champions. Wings 23 rooftop restaurant and pub promoted by Yaavi Ventures Limited offers panoramic views of Eldoret town, sparkling Sisibo, Elgon view estate and beyond as far as your eyes can capture from the top of one of the tallest building in Kenya “The Eldoret Daima Towers’.

Join our Executive Chef and his strong and highly experienced team, for seasonally-rotating, family-style comfort food, bio-dynamic wines and award-winning cocktails with large format apothecaries. With seasonal freshness reflected in food & drink, this Eldoret rooftop restaurant serves locally-sourced, New cuisine within the relaxed ambience of a Great City of Champions. Open year-round.

Foods you love to taste

With a large and varied menu for sunrise breakfast, lunch and dinner, the Rooftop features fresh natural ingredients infused in simple cooking; from grilled meat and fish dishes, fresh salads and pastas, to a selection of mouth-watering desserts.

From our Afro-Continental exquisite Cuisines

Our redesigned Rooftop Restaurant menu is more contemporary, unique and has a healthy combination of veg & Non vegetarian, fusion of continental and African exclusive preparations, one of its key hallmarks is the availability of clean cooked, fresh produce with a minimum of complication. The concept is to enhance the integrity of the ingredients allowing the natural flavors to shine through. Choose from our extensive range of cocktails, craft beers, range of wines, to compliment the flavors of the menu. Our irresistible array of desserts is the perfect way to complete the ultimate dining experience. The goal of our team is to provide you with outstanding service coupled with exquisite flavors leading to a very special dining delight at the sky view of Uasin Gishu county.
